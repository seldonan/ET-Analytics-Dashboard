LinkedIn Metric Definitions
Impressions
The number of times your post was shown on LinkedIn. This number is an estimate and may not be precise.
Clicks
Clicks on your content, company name, or logo by a signed-in member. Excludes interactions (reposts, reactions, comments). Multiple clicks on a document count as one.
Reactions, Comments & Reposts
The number of reactions, comments, and times content was shared by members, including Direct Sponsored Content and video posts.
CTR (Click-Through Rate)
CTR = (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100. Shows how often viewers click after seeing your post.
Engagement Rate (ER)
Ratio of total interactions (clicks + reactions + comments + shares + follows) per impression on your post.
Total Absolute Followers
Total number of followers at the day of data export. Reconstructed backwards using daily follower gain data.
Followers Gained
Net new followers gained during the selected period. Includes organic, sponsored, and auto-invited followers.
Followers Gained / Day
Average daily follower growth = total followers gained ÷ number of days in the selected period.
Count of Posts
Total number of posts published during the selected period (Audience = All followers).
